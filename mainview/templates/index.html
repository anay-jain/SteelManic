ABOUT US

We are leading in exporting quality products .

Steel Manic is into the manufacturing of high quality and a diverse range of stainless steel Pet ware, Bar ware and House ware products since 2000. Now, our designs are in the great demand in international market.

Our company has an aim to bring distinctive Indian designs of Pet Ware, Bar Ware and Kitchen ware to the international market. Within a very short span of time we have established our credentials as the segment leaders.

Steel Manic has maintained its edge as a leading exporter of high quality products. With its high standards of design, quality, and value, “Steel Manic” is the brand of the future. The latest technique, creative and exclusive designs are our expertise and the finest quality products are rapidly growing international clientele.
