OUR IDEOLOGY

We are consistently working in the betterment of our company.

While selecting the raw material, we prefer to use such which passes the FDA Test/ LFGB Test, as it will make sure that products are safe for the pets. We keep the safety of pets in mind while selecting the raw material so that we can assure that our products are safe for the pets. Our company's priority is human safety, so we take the BSCI Certification for our factory to make sure that the facilities which are provided by us to our team members are able to meet international standard.

We offer innovative products for your pets, each reflecting our commitment to excellence. The Pet wares make feeding and providing water to your pet easier to serve them in a better way. We offer a complete range of various products with different colors & pantone shades and variety of customized designs. Our products are manufactured by using stainless steel to ensure longer life span. This is used to serve food to pets and is offered in various sizes.

We are on way on consistent improvement with the help of BSCI (Business Social Compliance Initiative) who has designed special programmes to improve working conditions for the suppliers of BSCI’s participating member companies.

Taking one more step towards our team betterment we also get certification from SEDEX approved. SEDEX is an innovative and effective supply chain management solution, helping you to reduce risk, protect your company reputation and improve supply chain practices.
